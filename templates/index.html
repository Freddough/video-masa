How Cookies Work

Browser Cookies
Select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.) to let the app read your active login sessions directly. This works for most browsers on most systems, but may not work with Arc or other Chromium forks on macOS due to encryption restrictions.

Custom Cookies
Upload a cookies.txt file exported from your browser. These are saved by name and persist across sessions, even after the app restarts. Great for Arc users or anyone whose browser option doesn't work.

When to use cookies
Some sites (like X/Twitter) require a logged-in session to serve video. If a download fails with a login error, cookies will fix it.

How to export cookies
Install a browser extension like Get cookies.txt LOCALLY, navigate to the site you need (e.g. x.com while logged in), click the extension, and export. Then select Upload New… in the Cookies dropdown.
